My circuitry projects

My first robot

The first robot I have ever built was one designed to recognize distances between objects and avoid them all while moving forward. I used an infared sensor, two brushless motors, a motor controler, a servo to swivel the head, a circuit board, arduino board, a 12 volt battery pack, and an aluminum frame to build the robot.

EMP

I had once buit an Electro Magnetic Pulse generator able to generate around 1,000 teslas of alternating pulsing electromagnetic feilds. I had used a miniture high voltage pulse generator able to produce up to 500,000 volts of elecrteicity from a 12 volt DC battery pack. I hooked it up to a coil of copper wire along with a spark gap to act as a capacitor. To focus the feild produced by the coil I used an aluminum cone since recent studies have shown that a cone is most efficient in focusing an electromagnetic feild.

Light Song

For an engineering class I hooked up multicolord LEDs to a circuit board and programmed them to light up with a song I played on my homemade speaker. I had to make the speaker myself out of cups, magnets and copper wires. I also had to write the program to make the song myself, painstakingly converting piano notes to Hertz and millisecond pauses.
