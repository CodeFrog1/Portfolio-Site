My Glazing career

The first window I replaced

The first job I have ever done was replacing a wood window that had rotted out of its frame.
With the window in such a condition it was a pain getting it out of the frame along with the clean up.
But in the end with the help of my father we were able to install and seal up a new one making it as good as new.

The hardest window I have ever replaced

The hardest window I have ever replaced was one that was considerably high up in Hotel Park city. It was also the biggest window I have ever replaced so we had to rent a lift to install the window. It took us 6 hours to completely install and seal.

The easiest window I have ever replaced

The easiest window I have ever replaced was a relatively small vinal window that took only about 30 minutes to replace. With only spray foam and a wood frame keeping it in, it took the least amount of effort to complete.
