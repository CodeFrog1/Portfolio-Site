My personal hobbies

Skiing

One of my hobbies in the winter is going skiing. More specifically I backcountry ski, driving up to a hill side and hiking up it in our ski's. I'd also hike along snow trails and ski all the way down when I was done.

3d printing

I use a 3d printer to print out designs I make on CAD programs. One notable design I have recently printed was the dark saber from the Mandalorian. It took 5 days to design and print, but it was worth it in the end.

Building computers

I have little money so when making my own desktop computer I use spare parts from old computers and get them working on a new motherboard. Now with only a few tweaks it runs just as smoothly as any brand new computer.
